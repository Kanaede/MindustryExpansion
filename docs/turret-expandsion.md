## About Turret Expansion

Turret Expansion is a mod part of the Expansion series, Created by [Artemwing Zuoran](https://github.com/zuoranartemwing) and Contributor. 
The Turrets Expansion adds more Turrets for a better experience, more style variants, and different damage types.

Not based on reality, we made some turrets from imagination and ideas, not limited by reality.
